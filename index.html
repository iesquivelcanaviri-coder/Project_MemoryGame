JavaScript Memory Game

Match all pairs with the fewest moves and lowest time. Use the controls below to start.

How to Play

  • You may flip two cards per turn.
  • A card that is already face-up cannot be flipped again in the same turn.
  • If the two cards match, they remain visible.
  • If they do not match, they flip back automatically after a short delay.
  • Unmatched cards may be flipped again in future turns.
  • Matched cards cannot be flipped again.
  • Each pair of flips counts as one move.
  • Rapid clicking is disabled while cards reset.
  • The timer starts when the game begins and stops when all pairs are matched.
